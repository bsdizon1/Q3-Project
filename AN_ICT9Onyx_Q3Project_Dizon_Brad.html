Most Common Tips/ preperations for Fire Prevention


Several fire departments are responsible for fire prevention. The purpose of fire prevention is to educate the public on how to prevent potentially hazardous fires and how to survive these fires if they do occur. It is a proactive approach of preventing fires and minimizing the damage they cause.

  Install Smoke Alarms Put smoke detectors on each level of your home. Replace batteries frequently to test if it still works during fire drills.
  Cooking Safety While you're frying or grilling meals, stay in the kitchen. If you are not going to use the stove, turn it off.
  Electrical Safety Don't overload electrical outlets or extension cords.
  Escape Plan Have a fire escape plan and practice it with your family. Know at least two ways out of every room.
  Extinguisher When a portable fire extinguisher is present, the blaze is more likely to be controlled and less likely to spread out of control, increasing the likelihood of escape.
